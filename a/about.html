Aim
An organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and share their knowledge in the field of Data Science.The Data Science club of GNDEC, Ludhiana aims to create a collaborative and professional space for like-minded students looking to take advantage of the power of data to innovate and make a positive impact in the world. Our goal is to promote the interaction between industry professionals and our students to develop and nurture relationships via collaboration, training and the creation of new opportunities. To bridge the sizable gap between aspiring college students who dream of a Data Science career and the limited talent availability plaguing India's vast Data Science industry. The Data Science Club’s primary objective is to unify students across various branches who want to pursue Data Science as a desirable career path to their students by getting them to join our revolutionary career-oriented corporate club.

Goals
  1. To provide a learning opportunity for students, enhancing their capabilities by learning from various sources.
  2. Provides a platform to showcase your skills and enhances one's personality.
  3. Transforming ideas from planning phase to execution.
  4. Create the perfect path to success in the Data Science industry for aspiring students.
  5. Swift exchange of ideas, information and knowledge pertaining to Data Science among club members across the country
  6. Ensure that every student receives a thoroughly fulfilling learning experience that involves industry-approved cutting-edge Data Science practices and technological tools.
  7. Facilitate knowledge sharing of research and project data between a vast student network, with the aim of addressing India’s growing shortage of Data Scientists.
