Data Science Club

The Data Science Club is an organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and share their knowledge in the field of Data Science.

Overview

A group of people coming together to contribute and work in the field of Data Science at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana. Our team aim is to develop the needed skills needed in the mainly data science field.

Events
HACKOVERFLOW

HackOverflow event is organised to celebrate Hacktober Fest.
Participants completing the tasks will be provided with swag from Digital Ocean. Top 3 participants which make the most pull requests will be provided with a virtual swag kit from Data Science Club.
In this event, A github repository link will be provided to all the registered participants. Then, Participants will have to fork that repository. After that, Participants will have to send 4 pull requests by making files related to Data Science, Machine Learning, Python, C or CPP.
