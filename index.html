Data Science Club

The Data Science Club is an organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and share their knowledge in the field of Data Science.

Overview

We are a group of people coming together to contribute and work in the field of Data Science at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana. Our team aim is to develop the needed skills needed in the mainly data science field.
