Data Science Club

The Data Science Club is an organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and share their knowledge in the field of Data Science.

Overview

We are a group of people coming together to contribute and work in the field of Data Science at Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Ludhiana. Our team aim is to develop the needed skills needed in the mainly data science field.

Events
Workshop on Advanced Web Technologies

The workshop focused on role of advanced web technologies in shaping the world, data exploration and pre-processing, presenting the emerging research trends in data analytics. Eminent expert Ms.Minakshi Arora, Technical Lead Full Stack Developer from AppSmart Pvt. Ltd. Chandigarh presented her talk on web technologies and demonstrated live projects in the field of Web Technologies. Expert also shared industry knowledge related with topics like HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, Crypto currency, Node JS, Machine Learning, and Image Recognition etc. The workshop emphasized on emerging trends in advanced web technologies which facilitates the working of data science activities and provided guidelines to explore the area of data analytics with web technologies.

Past Event
Seminar on Data Analytics and Information Security

Data Science Club of GNDEC organized expert lecture on "Data Analytics and Information Security". Advance topics related with Information Security and Data Analytics in modern world, AI Case Studies etc. were presented. Mr.Ravi Mittal, Data Scientist shed light on the procedure of Data Analysis with python language. He also discussed various challenges faced by data scientists during data analysis activities. Dr.Sumit Kumar, Scientist presented various information security techniques with their applications and challenges in real world scenario. Participants discussed their queries with experts and gained knowledge about Data Science aspects.
